Alignment

Alignment is the placement of visual elements so they line up in a composition. In design, we use alignment or group elements.

Spacing

Spacing refers to the way that typing or printing is arranged on a page, especially in relation to the amount of space that is left between words or lines.

Uniformity

Uniformity the quality or fact of being the same, or of not changing or being different in any way,form or fashion with respect form, shape, size and color

Properties

Property is any item that a person or a business has legal title over. Property can be tangible items, such as houses, cars, or appliances, or it can refer to intangible items

Layouts

Layout refers to the arrangement of visual elements on a grid to convey a specific message. If the layout is difficult to read, then the design is ineffectively presented.

Designs

A design is the concept of or proposal for an object, process, or system. The word, design, refers to something that is or has been intentionally created by a thinking agent.
